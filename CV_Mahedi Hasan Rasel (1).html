"Efficient Diabetes Prediction by Employing Machine Learning
Algorithms "
Bagging decision trees is a machine learning method for detecting diabetes
based on physical conditions by combining the predictions of multiple
decision trees trained on random subsets of the data. This method can
improve accuracy and reduce variance and overfitting compared to a single
decision tree, but it is important to consider ethical and legal implications and
validate results when using it for medical diagnosis.
